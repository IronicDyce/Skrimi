FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is web scraping legal?

We collect only publicly available information — data anyone can view without logging in — and we respect takedown requests. We don't access password-protected areas or harvest personal data. You're responsible for ensuring your use of the delivered data complies with applicable laws and the source's terms.

What if a website changes and the scraper breaks?

On Business (recurring) plans we monitor your feeds and fix breakage as part of the subscription — your data keeps flowing. For one-time scrapes, we deliver a validated dataset at the time of the pull.

How do I receive the data?

However works for you — CSV, JSON, a Google Sheet, an S3 drop, or a dedicated API endpoint.

How fast is turnaround?

Most one-time scrapes are delivered within a few business days, depending on complexity. We'll give you a timeline with your free quote.

What does it cost?

On-Demand starts at $400 per site; Business plans start at $200/mo per site — both include up to 10,000 pages. Listed rates are baseline estimates for easy-to-medium sites; final pricing depends on scope and complexity.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes — we're happy to sign a mutual NDA before discussing sensitive projects.
